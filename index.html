Our Products

Premium quality construction materials for all your building and infrastructure needs

River Sand

Premium natural river sand ideal for plastering and masonry work. Clean, well-graded material for superior finish quality.

Usage: Plastering & Masonry

M-Sand (Manufactured Sand)

High-quality manufactured sand perfect for RCC and concrete applications. Consistent grading and superior strength properties.

Usage: RCC & Concrete

Aggregates (10mm / 20mm / 40mm)

High-strength construction aggregates in various sizes. Properly graded crushed stone for maximum concrete strength.

Usage: Concrete & RCC Work

Stone Chips

Clean, durable crushed stone chips with excellent bonding properties. Angular shape ensures superior concrete strength.

Usage: Concrete & Foundation

GSB & Filling Material

Granular sub-base and filling materials specifically designed for road construction and infrastructure projects.

Usage: Road & Infrastructure
